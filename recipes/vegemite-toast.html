Vegemite Toast

Return Home

Description

The ultimate, life-giving vegemite recipe. It's the warm blanket on a cold winter's day, the refreshing soda on a hot summer's day - it's vegemite on toast!

Ingredients

  • 2 slices bread, preferable white
  • 1/8 teaspoon vegemite, more if you are game
  • 1/2 teaspoon butter or 1/2 teaspoon margarine, Generous serve is recommended

Instructions

  1. Toast your favourite variety of bread
  2. Butter it generously
  3. Spread a thin layer of Vegemite on it. (Reminder, this is very flavoursome stuff so you don't need to use much at all).
